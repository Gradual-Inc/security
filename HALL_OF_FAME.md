# Security Hall of Fame

Gradual thanks the following security researchers for reporting vulnerabilities
to us in good faith under our [Security Policy](./README.md).

Researchers are listed in order of first valid report, grouped by year. Credit
is given under the name or handle each researcher requested. Researchers who
declined public credit are not listed.

## How to get listed

Submit a valid vulnerability report following our [Security Policy](./README.md).
Once the issue is triaged and remediated, we will add you here under the name
or handle you specified in your report (unless you asked to remain anonymous).
